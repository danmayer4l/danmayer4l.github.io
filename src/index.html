About Danny Mayer

Designer | Marketer | Blogger | Creative

I am a front end web developer and digital marketer.

From a young age, I had always put all my passion and effort into whatever creative endeavor I was doing at that moment, whether it was music, design, photography, or writing. I beleive in breaking down the barriers in your area of expertise as a way of improving your thinking towards the subject. Having years of experience in creative mediums, a drive for absolute perfectionism, and a love for technology drove me towards web design and development as a way to satisfy all the different aspects of my passions.

I take a minimalistic approach in my work and strive to approach every project with the needs of the consumer and their target audience at the forefront, while still providing a fresh and unique experience for every project. I believe in getting the job done right the first time. A persons website, content, product or service, and brand are their digital representation of themselves and so often you see a lackluster approach taken towards these important assests. Lets work together to make sure you are putting your best presence out there.
